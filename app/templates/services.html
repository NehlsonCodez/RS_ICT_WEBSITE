What We Do
Comprehensive ICT Services for Rivers State

The Rivers State ICT Department delivers a wide portfolio of technology services to government ministries, departments, agencies (MDAs), and the general public. From e-government platforms and broadband infrastructure to citizen digital skills training, our services are designed to make Rivers State a model of technology-enabled governance in Nigeria.

🖥️

E-Government Platform Management

We develop and maintain digital portals that allow Rivers State residents to access government services online — from birth certificates and land registry queries to business permits and tax payments — without visiting a government office.

🌐

ICT Infrastructure & Connectivity

We coordinate the deployment and management of broadband networks, fibre-optic connectivity, and Wi-Fi hotspots across government buildings, public spaces, and educational institutions in all 23 LGAs of Rivers State.

🔒

Cybersecurity & Data Protection

Our cybersecurity unit safeguards all state government digital assets, implements Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) compliance across MDAs, and conducts regular security audits, staff awareness campaigns, and incident response operations.

📡

Government Cloud & Data Centre

The Rivers State Government Data Centre, managed by our department, hosts critical applications for MDAs including financial management systems, personnel records, and citizen databases — with 99.9% uptime SLA and disaster recovery protocols.

🎓

Digital Skills Training

Through the Rivers State ICT Training Center, we deliver free and subsidised training in web development, data analysis, cybersecurity, AI tools, cloud computing, graphic design, and digital marketing — targeting youth, women, PWDs, and civil servants.

📊

ICT Policy & Standards

We develop and enforce Rivers State ICT policy frameworks, enterprise architecture standards, and technology procurement guidelines to ensure all government technology investments are interoperable, secure, and cost-effective.

🤝

Tech Ecosystem Development

We partner with tech hubs, startups, universities, and private sector operators to grow the Rivers State technology ecosystem — supporting innovation, creating youth employment, and attracting technology investment to Port Harcourt and beyond.

📱

Citizen Digital Helpdesk

Our walk-in and telephone ICT helpdesk at ICT House, Trans-Amadi assists citizens who need help accessing e-government services, troubleshooting devices, and understanding digital tools. Available Monday to Friday, 8am–5pm.
