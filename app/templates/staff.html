Director General

Engr. Chidiebere Nwosu

BSc. Computer Science (UNIPORT) · MSc. ICT Policy (University of Leeds, UK) · CISA · PMP

Engr. Nwosu was appointed Director General in 2021 by Governor Siminalayi Fubara. With over 22 years of experience in ICT management and policy, he previously served as Head of IT at the Rivers State Ministry of Finance and ICT Consultant at the Federal Ministry of Communications. Under his leadership, the department has trained over 8,000 residents and deployed the Rivers State Government Service Bus — integrating 18 MDAs on a common digital platform.
