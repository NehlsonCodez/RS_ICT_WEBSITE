Rivers State Launches Broadband Connectivity Project for 200 Schools

Governor Siminalayi Fubara has commissioned the Rivers State School Connectivity Initiative, a project that will connect 200 public secondary schools across all 23 LGAs to high-speed internet by Q4 2026. The project, implemented by the ICT Department in partnership with MTN Nigeria and Galaxy Backbone, will deploy Wi-Fi in school libraries, science labs, and ICT classrooms.

Rivers State NDPR Compliance Audit Completed for All MDAs

The ICT Department has completed its first full Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) compliance audit across all 48 MDAs in Rivers State. Audit reports have been submitted to the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA) as required.
