About the Center

Rivers State's Premier Public Technology Learning Hub

Established in 2012 and upgraded in 2023, the Rivers State ICT Training Center is the flagship public technology education facility of the Rivers State Government. Located at ICT House, Trans-Amadi Industrial Layout, Port Harcourt, the center provides world-class digital skills training to residents from all 23 local government areas.

With a capacity of 400 students per cohort, the center runs morning, afternoon, and weekend sessions to accommodate working professionals, students, and full-time trainees. Since inception, over 15,000 Rivers State residents have been trained and certified.

The center operates in partnership with the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA), Microsoft Nigeria, and Google for Education — ensuring our curriculum meets international industry standards.

Certification
What You Earn at the End

Participants who complete a program and pass the final assessment receive a Rivers State Government Digital Skills Certificate, signed by the Director General of the ICT Department. The certificate is registered in the RSG Digital Skills Database, which employers can verify online.

Advanced program graduates also receive preparation for globally recognised certifications including CompTIA Security+, AWS Cloud Practitioner, and Microsoft Azure Fundamentals — with the ICT Department subsidising exam vouchers for qualifying completers.
