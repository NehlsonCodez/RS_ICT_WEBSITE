Who We Are

Transforming Rivers State, One Click at a Time

The Rivers State ICT Department is the principal government agency responsible for planning, implementing, and coordinating information and communication technology initiatives across all ministries, departments, and agencies (MDAs) of Rivers State.

Established under the Ministry of Science, Technology and Innovation, we drive digital transformation, e-government adoption, and ICT capacity building — positioning Rivers State as the foremost tech-enabled state in Southern Nigeria.

Our flagship Rivers State ICT Training Center has trained over 15,000 citizens across 23 LGAs in digital skills from basic computing to AI and cloud technology.

"Harnessing technology as the great equalizer — making quality digital education accessible to every Rivers State resident."

— Director General, Rivers State ICT Department
