Scholarships & Subsidies

No One Gets Left Behind

The Rivers State Government provides full scholarships for youth aged 16–25 from low-income households, persons with disabilities (PWDs), and women re-entering the workforce. Up to 30% of each cohort is reserved for scholarship recipients.

To apply for a scholarship, indicate "Scholarship Request" in the referral field during registration, or visit the ICT Department office with a community development letter or means test documentation.

Eligibility for Free / Subsidised Programs

  • Rivers State residents aged 16 and above
  • Youth (16–25): all beginner programs are free
  • Civil servants: subsidised rates on all programs
  • Women: additional 20% subsidy on advanced programs
  • PWDs: full scholarship on all programs
  • Valid RSG-issued ID or LGA attestation required
